Hospital Occupancy Data from ECDC

The below chart shows hospital occupancy over time (NOT daily hospitalizations) for the countries for which data have been provided by the European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control.   Also note that this is a record of hospitalized patients for whom testing for covid 19 was positive, not of patients who were hospitalized directly because of covid 19. This graph also does not indicate occupancy as a percentage of total available beds.   From what I could determine, it is actually fairly typical for rates to be above 90% (or even exceed total available) during some seasons (occupancy rate by year for some European regions)
