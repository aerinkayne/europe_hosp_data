Hospital Occupancy Data from ECDC

The below chart shows hospital occupancy over time (not daily hospitalizations) for the countries for which data have been provided for download by the European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control.  It is a record of hospitalized patients for whom testing for covid 19 was positive, not of patients who were hospitalized directly because of covid 19.

Some countries have more than one peak because both hospital and ICU occupancy are charted.  Data points have tooltip info on the desktop view.
